About
D3d12infoGUI is a tool for querying support for various Direct3D 12 features.
In this report you can find data about all D3D12 capable GPUs in your local machine.
There's also community-driven online database listing same information for various hardware which you can open via "Online Database" link in the header.
You can submit your reports to the online database with one of the submit buttons if database doesn't contain equivalent data already.
If database contains equivalent report, or if you just submitted one of your reports, you can then open it online and share the link to your report.
Those reports don't contain any personal information, you can see full contents of those reports on this page before submitting.
